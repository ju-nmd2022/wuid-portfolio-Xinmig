About the city I live.

Now I study and living in Jönköping.

It is a very big and calm city. It alos have a great view with lake.

He doesn't make people feel crowded, even on weekends and holidays. That one of the reasons why I like it.


Good places to go out

There are some places that I will recommend for go out.

First is the downtown commercial street, most of the restaurants and stores are in the city center. And in the commercial street, there are a lot of stores about closeing and even cosmetics. It is close to the main station too, so the transportation is convenient there.

The second one is a shopping mall called Acecs, it is a little far from the city center, but it has more shops than the commercial street. And it is besides the Ikea and other big supermarkt, that area contains a wide variety of large stores for you to choose from.


Finally, it's about the food

In Jönköping you can find the following types of cuisine: Chinese food, Japanese food, fast food, Swedish food, American food, Lebanese food, Indo-Pakistani food and Italian food.

Most of the restaurants are in the city center. And my favorite is those Pizza restaurants. I relly love their delicious thin-bottomed pizza.

If you are interested in this city, then don't hesitate to come!
